About Me

I decided that I want to do full stack web development for my career, during a time where I did an internship and part time work in web development for a startup company while I was still doing my studies.

I was lucky to be able to join another early startup shortly before graduation and worked in a small team of four developers. We did a wide range of web-related projects, for clients and also for our own product, and had the valuable experience to be involved in the whole software development cycle, from project ideation and implementation, to deployment and maintenance.

I enjoy building things that not only bring self-fulfillment, but also bring value to other people. Apart from web development, my other passion is songwriting and music production. Similar to how I develop the web, I also take a DIY approach when it comes to my music. I aspire to be both a software engineer and a music artist that creates great software and music respectively.
